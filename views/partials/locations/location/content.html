About the Hospital

For more than 120 years, The Christ Hospital has been a leader in medical excellence. And now, we’ve grown to become more than a hospital. Today, The Christ Hospital is a vast network of physicians and staff, working together to make superior medicine convenient and accessible. This network - known as The Christ Hospital Health Network - includes The Christ Hospital, a 555-bed, not-for-profit acute care facility in Cincinnati, Ohio. We offer a wide range of medical, surgical and testing services at our main campus in the urban core of Mt. Auburn and at more than 100 outpatient & Physician practice locations throughout Greater Cincinnati. We are consistently recognized by U.S. News & World Report as one of the nation’s top hospitals; have been named Cincinnati’s Most Preferred Hospital for 17 consecutive years by National Research Corporation (NRC); and have been granted  Magent® recognition from the American Nurses Credentialing Center for excellence in nursing care.
